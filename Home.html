This webpage focuses on protecting the environment and the nature mainly in the issue of the burning of Amazon. The Amazon consists of thousands of plant and animals species, some of them were endemic in the forest. The Amazon rainforest produces roughly 6% of the world's oxygen, 6% might be small a number but the safety of all living beings is at risk. Global warming and Climate change can affect the lifestyle of the living beings. Negligence of this issue might result to worse consequences we will face later on.

MISSION:

Our mission for this website is to open the minds of people neglecting their obligations to protect Mother Earth, the people benefitting from the environment, and the flora and fauna living around us. To spread awareness for the possible consequences of recklessness of our actions.

VISSION:

We vision ourselves spreading awareness to our people for the sake of bringing safety to our environment, and all living creatures.
